Your 2010 to 2025 dictionary!

A semi-community project started by @FloorMann

Contributing

Want to contribute? Check out these cards below to determine what you can do to help!

Suggest a word

Have a word you want to add? Head over to our GitHub repo and open a new issue with the word you want to add, along with its description, synonyms, and antonyms!

Develop the website

Know Python, or HTML/CSS/JavaScript? Check out our GitHub repo! You can clone the repo, add your changes, test, and make a pull request. Our word lister is done in Python templating, and the rest is done in plain HTML/CSS/JavaScript.

Accept/decline words

Know your modern-day slang? Email us and inquire about accepting/declining word requests yourself! If you have been doing this for a good amount of time, you will be promoted to refining accepted requests and making them more clear.
